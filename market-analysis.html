Where Garry works

Active across the North Shore, with deep local knowledge of these communities.

Beach Haven

A friendly, water-fringed community with strong first-home and family appeal, and good value relative to neighbouring suburbs.

Unsworth Heights

Popular with families for its schooling, motorway access and mix of standalone and cross-lease homes.

Glenfield

Central, well-connected and diverse — a steady entry point to the North Shore market.

Windsor Park

Quiet, leafy streets close to Albany amenities, parks and the East Coast Bays.

What's shaping the market

Auckland's residential market moves with interest rates, lending settings, migration and the balance of listings against active buyers. On the North Shore, well-presented family homes in good school zones continue to attract the most competition, while buyers remain price-conscious and selective.

For sellers, that means presentation, accurate pricing and strong marketing matter more than ever. For buyers, finance pre-approval and moving decisively on the right property are key. Whatever the headlines say, the most useful number is a current, evidence-based appraisal of your specific property.
