Licensed Real Estate Salesperson at Barfoot & Thompson — helping New Zealanders buy, sell and invest with confidence.

Home / About

Your trusted real estate partner

I'm Garry Singh, a Licensed Salesperson with Barfoot & Thompson at the Beach Haven branch. I'm dedicated to being your trusted guide through the real estate journey — whether you're buying, selling or investing on Auckland's North Shore — ensuring a seamless and rewarding experience every step of the way.

I completed a Civil Engineering Diploma in India and arrived in New Zealand nearly seven years ago. Since then my path has spanned hospitality, customer service and retail sales, each role sharpening the communication and negotiation skills I now bring to real estate. I chose Barfoot & Thompson for its stellar reputation and the outstanding marketing, IT and training support behind every salesperson.

Guided by integrity and a genuine commitment to client satisfaction, I'd love nothing more than to help you find that special place you can call home. I speak English, Hindi and Punjabi, and I'm licensed under the Real Estate Agents Act 2008.
